## Summary
TerraFlow's technology approach is scientifically sound with early validation at lab scale. However, significant technical risks remain as the company moves toward pilot demonstration. The technology combines proven concepts with novel innovations that require field validation.

2. **Wellbore Design:** Closed-loop heat exchanger configuration
3. **Working Fluid:** Supercritical CO2 heat transfer medium

## Technical Claims Assessment

### Claim 1: "40% drilling cost reduction vs. conventional"
- **Assessment:** Plausible but unproven at scale
- **Evidence:** Lab tests show 3x faster penetration in granite
- **Concern:** MM-wave performance at 3km+ depth unknown
- **Validation Needed:** Pilot drilling results

### Claim 2: "15% higher thermal efficiency vs. water-based systems"
- **Assessment:** Theoretically sound
- **Evidence:** sCO2 properties well-documented; company's simulations validated by NREL
- **Concern:** Real-world losses may reduce advantage
- **Validation Needed:** Pilot operational data

### Claim 3: "90% of US locations accessible"
- **Assessment:** Aggressive but directionally correct
- **Evidence:** DOE enhanced geothermal maps support broad potential
- **Concern:** Economic viability varies by location; "accessible" ≠ "economical"
- **Validation Needed:** Detailed cost modeling post-pilot

## Key Technical Risks

### Risk 1: Millimeter-Wave Drilling Scale-Up (HIGH)
- **Issue:** Technology unproven at required depths (3,000m+)
- **Failure Mode:** Beam attenuation, rock debris interference
- **Mitigation:** Phased drilling with validation checkpoints
- **Impact if Realized:** Project delays, cost overruns, potential pivot

### Risk 2: Wellbore Integrity (MEDIUM-HIGH)
- **Issue:** Long-term integrity of closed-loop system at depth
- **Failure Mode:** Casing failure, thermal stress fractures
- **Mitigation:** Conservative design factors, materials testing
- **Impact if Realized:** Maintenance costs, production losses

### Risk 3: Thermal Drawdown (MEDIUM)
- **Issue:** Heat extraction rate vs. natural replenishment
- **Failure Mode:** Declining output over time
- **Mitigation:** Modeling validated by academic partners
- **Impact if Realized:** Reduced long-term economics

### Risk 4: sCO2 System Performance (MEDIUM)
- **Issue:** Managing high-pressure sCO2 loop at scale
- **Failure Mode:** Leaks, compressor issues, corrosion
- **Mitigation:** Leveraging existing sCO2 industrial experience
- **Impact if Realized:** Efficiency losses, maintenance costs

## Conclusion
