# Nexus AI Partners with Notion to Power AI Features Across Platform

**Source:** TechCrunch
**Date:** January 15, 2026
**Author:** Connie Loizos

---

Nexus AI, the San Francisco-based AI infrastructure startup, announced today a significant expansion of its partnership with Notion, the popular productivity software company.

Under the expanded agreement, Nexus will power all of Notion's AI features across its platform, managing traffic between multiple large language model providers including OpenAI, Anthropic, and Cohere. The deal represents one of the largest production deployments for the Series A company.

"Nexus has been instrumental in helping us deliver reliable AI features to our users," said Notion's VP of Engineering in a prepared statement. "Their intelligent routing has reduced our AI infrastructure costs by 40% while improving response times."

The partnership comes at a critical time for Nexus, which is reportedly in conversations for a Series B round. Sources familiar with the matter suggest the company is seeing strong inbound interest from growth-stage investors, though the company declined to comment on fundraising plans.

Nexus AI raised a $14 million Series A led by Andreessen Horowitz in October 2025. The company's platform helps enterprises manage the complexity of working with multiple AI model providers through a unified API gateway.

The expanded Notion partnership follows similar production deployments with Cursor and Scale AI, positioning Nexus as an emerging leader in the LLM infrastructure space.

Industry analysts note that the timing is significant given increased competition in the space. AWS recently announced preview features for its AI Gateway service, while Google Cloud has been expanding its Vertex AI platform with similar capabilities.

"The race is on to become the standard infrastructure layer for enterprise AI," said Matt Murphy, an analyst at Menlo Ventures. "Nexus has a head start with proven production scale, but the cloud providers are coming."
