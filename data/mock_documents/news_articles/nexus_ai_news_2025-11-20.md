# Nexus AI Achieves SOC 2 Type II Certification, Eyes Enterprise Expansion

**Source:** VentureBeat
**Date:** November 20, 2025
**Author:** Michael Nuñez

---

Nexus AI announced today that it has achieved SOC 2 Type II certification, a key milestone that positions the AI infrastructure startup to accelerate its push into enterprise accounts.

The certification, which validates the company's security controls over an extended audit period, is often a prerequisite for deals with large enterprises and regulated industries. The company completed the audit in under six months, faster than typical timelines.

"Enterprise buyers increasingly require SOC 2 Type II before they'll move to production," said VP of Sales James Park. "This certification removes a major barrier in our sales process and opens up conversations with financial services and healthcare companies."

The announcement comes as Nexus reports strong momentum in its enterprise segment. The company shared that it now has 12 customers with annual contract values exceeding $50,000, up from just three at the start of the year.

"We're seeing a clear pattern where companies start with our self-serve product and quickly expand as they realize the complexity of managing multiple LLM providers," said CEO Dr. Sarah Chen in an interview. "Our largest customer is now doing over 100 million API calls per month through our gateway."

The company also announced the opening of a new office in New York to support its growing East Coast customer base, with plans to hire 15 people in sales and customer success roles by Q2 2026.

Industry watchers note that the enterprise focus represents a strategic bet during a time of increasing competition in the LLM infrastructure space. While some competitors focus on developer-first, bottom-up adoption, Nexus appears to be building for top-down enterprise sales.
