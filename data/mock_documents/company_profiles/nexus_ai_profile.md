# Nexus AI - Company Profile

## Overview
**Company Name:** Nexus AI
**Founded:** 2022
**Headquarters:** San Francisco, CA
**Stage:** Series A
**Last Valuation:** $85M (October 2025)
**Total Funding:** $18M

## Company Description
Nexus AI is building enterprise-grade AI infrastructure for deploying and managing large language models at scale. Their platform enables companies to run multiple LLMs with unified APIs, automatic failover, intelligent routing, and comprehensive observability.

## Leadership Team
- **Dr. Sarah Chen** - CEO & Co-founder. Former Principal Engineer at Google DeepMind. PhD in Machine Learning from Stanford.
- **Marcus Williams** - CTO & Co-founder. Ex-Stripe infrastructure lead. Built payment processing systems handling $50B+ annually.
- **Priya Sharma** - VP Engineering. Former Netflix senior architect. Expert in distributed systems.
- **James Park** - VP Sales. Previously led enterprise sales at Datadog.

## Product
**Core Product: Nexus Gateway**
- Unified API gateway for 20+ LLM providers (OpenAI, Anthropic, Cohere, etc.)
- Intelligent request routing based on latency, cost, and capability requirements
- Built-in caching, rate limiting, and semantic deduplication
- Real-time cost tracking and budget controls
- SOC 2 Type II certified

**New Product (Beta): Nexus Orchestrator**
- Multi-agent workflow builder
- Visual pipeline editor for complex AI workflows
- Currently in private beta with 15 design partners
