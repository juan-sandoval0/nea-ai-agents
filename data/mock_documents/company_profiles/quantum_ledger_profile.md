# Quantum Ledger - Company Profile

## Overview
**Company Name:** Quantum Ledger
**Founded:** 2021
**Headquarters:** New York, NY
**Stage:** Series B
**Last Valuation:** $340M (June 2025)
**Total Funding:** $78M

## Company Description
Quantum Ledger provides real-time payment infrastructure for cross-border B2B transactions. The platform combines traditional banking rails with blockchain settlement to reduce cross-border payment times from 3-5 days to under 4 hours while cutting costs by up to 70%.

## Leadership Team
- **Michael Okonkwo** - CEO & Co-founder. Former Goldman Sachs VP, led international payments division. MBA from Wharton.
- **Dr. Elena Vasquez** - CTO & Co-founder. Ex-Coinbase principal engineer. PhD in Distributed Systems from MIT.
- **Robert Kim** - CFO. Former Square finance director. CPA with Big Four background.
- **Jennifer Walsh** - Chief Compliance Officer. 20 years in financial services compliance, former FINRA examiner.
- **David Adebayo** - VP Sales. Former Stripe enterprise sales leader, Africa/EMEA focus.

## Product
**Core Product: QuantumRail**
- Real-time B2B payment processing for international transactions
- Hybrid settlement (traditional rails + blockchain for speed)
- Multi-currency support (42 currencies live)
- Automated FX with competitive rates
- Full audit trail and compliance reporting

**Product Extensions:**
- **QuantumConnect:** API for ERP/accounting software integration
- **QuantumInsights:** Cash flow forecasting and analytics
- **QuantumCredit:** Trade financing (launched Q4 2025)

- **Money Transmitter Licenses:** 38 US states (4 pending)

## Key Risks
1. **Regulatory uncertainty** - NY DFS inquiry ongoing, potential for increased compliance costs
2. **Competitive pressure** - Well-funded competitors (Wise, Airwallex) expanding B2B offerings
3. **Blockchain regulatory clarity** - Evolving regulations around crypto/blockchain could impact settlement method
4. **Key corridor concentration** - Heavy reliance on US-Mexico corridor

## Investment Thesis
Large TAM with clear inefficiencies in legacy systems, strong technical team combining fintech and crypto expertise, proven ability to navigate complex regulatory environment. Growing revenue with path to profitability. Main concerns are regulatory overhang and competitive dynamics.
