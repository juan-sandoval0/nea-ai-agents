# CodeLayer - Company Profile

## Overview
**Company Name:** CodeLayer
**Founded:** 2021
**Headquarters:** Seattle, WA
**Stage:** Series B
**Last Valuation:** $280M (March 2025)
**Total Funding:** $62M

## Company Description
CodeLayer provides a cloud-native internal developer platform (IDP) that simplifies infrastructure management for engineering teams. The platform abstracts away Kubernetes complexity, automates deployments, and provides a developer-friendly self-service interface—enabling companies to achieve "platform engineering" capabilities without building custom tooling.

## Product
**Core Product: CodeLayer Platform**
- Self-service developer portal (catalog, templates, golden paths)
- Automated Kubernetes deployment and management
- Environment provisioning (dev, staging, prod) in minutes
- Built-in observability and cost management
- Integrations with GitHub, GitLab, Backstage, Terraform, ArgoCD

**Key Features:**
1. **Service Catalog:** Discover and manage all services in one place
2. **Golden Paths:** Pre-approved deployment templates reducing errors
3. **Environment Management:** Spin up full environments with one click
4. **Cost Visibility:** Per-service cloud cost attribution
5. **Compliance:** Built-in policy enforcement and audit logging

## Key Risks
1. **Key person departure:** VP Engineering loss creates leadership gap during critical scaling phase
2. **Competitive market:** Well-funded competitors (Humanitec $30M, Port $30M) pursuing same market
3. **Open source threat:** Backstage gaining enterprise traction as free alternative
4. **Platform dependency:** Some customers may consolidate to cloud provider solutions

## Investment Thesis
CodeLayer addresses a genuine pain point for engineering organizations—Kubernetes complexity is a universal challenge. The company has strong product-market fit evidenced by high NRR and enterprise adoption. Key concerns are VP Engineering departure and increasing competition. The market is large (estimated $4B+ by 2027 for developer platforms) with room for multiple winners.
