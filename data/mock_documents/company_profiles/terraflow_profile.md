# TerraFlow - Company Profile

## Overview
**Company Name:** TerraFlow Energy
**Founded:** 2024
**Headquarters:** Denver, CO
**Stage:** Seed

## Company Description
TerraFlow Energy is developing next-generation geothermal technology that enables geothermal power generation in locations previously considered unsuitable. Their proprietary closed-loop system extracts heat from hot dry rock formations without requiring natural hydrothermal reservoirs, potentially making geothermal accessible in 90% of US locations.

## Technology
**Core Innovation: TerraLoop System**
- Closed-loop geothermal system using proprietary drilling and heat exchange technology
- Enables geothermal in hot dry rock formations (no water required)
- Smaller surface footprint than traditional geothermal
- Potential for 24/7 baseload clean energy at competitive costs

**Key Technical Advantages:**
1. **Drilling Innovation:** Millimeter-wave drilling technology reduces drilling costs by 40%
2. **Heat Exchange:** Proprietary supercritical CO2 working fluid with 15% higher thermal efficiency
3. **Modular Design:** Scalable from 5MW to 100MW installations
4. **Site Flexibility:** Works in crystalline basement rock, sedimentary basins, and volcanic regions

**Development Stage:**
- Lab-scale validation complete
- First pilot well drilling Q2 2026 (DOE co-funded)
- Commercial demonstration planned for 2027

## Key Risks
1. **Technology risk:** Pilot demonstration critical; unproven at commercial scale
2. **Long sales cycles:** Utility/energy sales cycles can be 2-5+ years
3. **Capital intensity:** Full commercial deployment requires significant CapEx
4. **Execution risk:** Small team, ambitious technical milestones
5. **Competitive risk:** Better-funded competitors advancing rapidly

## Investment Thesis
TerraFlow addresses a massive opportunity in baseload clean energy with differentiated technology. The team has deep technical expertise and early government validation through DOE funding. However, the company is early-stage with significant technology and execution risk. The pilot demonstration in 2026 will be critical for Series A positioning.

**Key Question:** Can TerraFlow differentiate technically vs. better-funded competitors like Fervo and Eavor?
